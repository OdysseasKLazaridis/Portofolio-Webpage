Hount
About me

Tattoos are more than just art; they’re a way to express who you are, what you’ve been through, and what you stand for. They’re the stories we wear on our skin, etched forever.

I go by Hount. My journey into the game started in Athens at 15, tagging walls with graffiti. By 20, I switched gears and jumped into the world of tattoos, captivated by the idea of creating something permanent on someone else’s body. That trust, that bond, still keeps me going. Over the years, I’ve put in the work—mastering the craft, fusing blackwork and abstract with semi-realism.

A tattoo isn’t just ink on skin; it’s a story, a statement, a way to own your confidence and put your soul on display. Every piece I do is more than just art—it’s a part of you, a chapter of your life, forever inked into history.
